ABOUT US!

BookHerd is a website dedicated to teachers, learners, and animal lovers.
We aim to entertain, educate, and provide a place for people to get the resources they
need such as school materials, plushies for entertainment, and to have fun.
We accomplish this through our many features, such as:

  • • E-library (Arranged by Grade Level)
  • • Animal Trivia
  • • Puzzles
  • • Riddles
  • • Crosswords
  • • Math Equations
  • • 4 Pics 1 Word
  • • School Products
  • • Plushies
